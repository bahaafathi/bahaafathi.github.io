Data Deletion Instruction

We do not save your personal data on our server. According to the Facebook Platform rules, we have to provide User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL. If you want to delete your activities for Move-mo, follow these instructions:

  1. Go to Your Facebook Account's Setting & Privacy. Click "Setting".
  2. Then, go to "Apps and Websites" and you will see all of your Apps activities.
  3. Select the option box for Move-mo.
  4. Click the "Remove" button.

If you wish to delete your user account data, you have to request us to delete your account. Subject to your Move-mo user account no longer being required in the future, please send your request with the account-registered email to admin@move-mo.online. Your account will be deleted, and all data will no longer be saved.
